I, Robin Bansal, am currently a final-year student pursuing a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering at the Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT) Nagpur, with an expected graduation date of June 2025. I have always been passionate about technology and its ability to solve real-world problems, and this passion has guided me through my academic and professional journey so far. I am currently working as Data Analyst Intern at Lloyds Logistics Pvt. Ltd.

Since joining IIIT Nagpur in 2021, I have immersed myself in the fields of data science, software engineering, and machine learning. My time here has been marked by continuous learning and the practical application of theoretical knowledge, shaping my technical abilities and problem-solving approach.

In July 2024, I started working as a Data Analyst intern at Lloyds Logistics Pvt. Ltd., where I am responsible for analyzing large datasets related to logistics and transportation. My work involves gathering, cleaning, and organizing data to optimize logistics operations. I develop detailed reports and visualizations using tools such as Power BI, Tableau, and Excel to present findings that contribute to enhancing the efficiency of the company’s supply chain management processes. This role has allowed me to apply advanced analytical techniques to real-world challenges and gain valuable insights into the logistics industry.

Prior to this, from May 2024 to July 2024, I completed a Project Internship at IIIT Guwahati, sponsored by the Science and Engineering Research Board (SERB). The project focused on optimizing edge service caching to reduce latency in networked systems. By implementing a solution that strategically divided cache between public and private storage, we achieved up to a 36.24% improvement in latency and reduced operational costs by over 83%. This project allowed me to dive deeper into advanced algorithms, multithreading, and system optimization, providing a strong foundation in real-time systems.

In addition to these internships, I have worked on several personal projects that reflect my growing expertise in machine learning, networked systems, and computer vision. One such project involved the development of an optimized data forwarding and cache management system for networked edge servers, utilizing Python and multithreading. This system significantly improved data handling efficiency and responsiveness across multiple servers. Another notable project was an image captioning system that combined the strengths of computer vision and natural language processing to generate descriptive captions for images, achieving commendable accuracy metrics on the Flickr8k dataset.

Beyond technical work, I have embraced leadership roles during my time at IIIT Nagpur. I am also an active member of the DotSlash Club, competitive programming club, where I collaborate with fellow students to enhance problem-solving skills and promote a culture of coding excellence on campus.

As I reflect on my journey, I am proud of the progress I have made. From competitive programming challenges on platforms like Codeforces and Codechef to contributing to large-scale research projects, I have continually sought to improve my technical and analytical skills. My interests span across various domains, including data science, machine learning, algorithms, and Programming, and I am constantly striving to apply these skills to new challenges.

Looking ahead, I am excited about the endless possibilities that lie within the tech industry. I aim to apply my knowledge and experiences to meaningful projects that can make a difference. Whether through further research or professional work, I am determined to contribute to advancements in technology that benefit society as a whole.

Thank you for taking the time to explore my portfolio. I hope to continue growing and learning, and I look forward to any opportunities that may come my way.
