Department of IITB


Our college takes immense pride in its exceptional faculty team. Our professors are not just educators but also industry experts, bringing a wealth of real-world experience to the classroom. They are passionate about nurturing the next generation of engineers, instilling a deep understanding of theoretical concepts and their practical applications. With a commitment to research, innovation, and staying up-to-date with the latest technological advancements, our faculty members are at the forefront of their respective fields.


Department of Basic Science

The department envisages to become a state-of-the-art department with high-quality education and cutting edge interdisciplinary research in science. It acts as a foundation for all the core engineering branches and offers courses in Applied Sciences, Mathematics, Humanities and Environmental Studies. The department continuously put valuable efforts to provide the students latest state-of-the-art curriculum. This unique course blends the best of contemporary Basic Sciences and Engineering to create professionals who are equally comfortable with both science and technology.
