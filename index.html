Director Message

A very warm greeting from International Institute of Technology, Butibori.

I, Robin Bansal, am fortunate to have an opportunity to get associated with IIT, Butibori as its Founder Director. I joined this coveted Institution of National Importance on 26th June 2018 and have witnessed its phenomenal growth since then.

When I look back, I could see the transformation happened in last few years from the humble start of IIT, Butibori in the Makeshift Campus to state-of-the-art sprawling 100 Acres Permanent Campus consisting of Air-Conditioned Class Rooms & Laboratories and soothing exteriors. It is true that full development of the campus is a long process and will take time. But I hope that in next few years the Campus shall be rated at par with any other Centrally Funded Institution.

From meagre 48 students in 2016-17 to more than 1400 students in 2022-23 and expected strength of 1800 students in 2023-24, the Institute has come a long way. The popularity of IIT, Butibori Read More...
